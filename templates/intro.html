Reddit Sentiment Analysis on AI & LLMs

This project analyzes Reddit discussions around Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Large Language Models (LLMs), helping users understand sentiment, trends, and public opinion in real time.

  • Step 1: Enter a subreddit focused on AI or LLM topics.
  • Step 2: Fetch top posts and perform sentiment analysis.
  • Step 3: Add, edit, or delete posts using built-in CRUD features.
  • Step 4: Explore sentiment trends and popular keywords.
